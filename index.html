About Me

An ever-evolving individual that is deeply in love with nature, photography, and visual design. An Eleven Fifty Academy graduate (Web Devlopment & UX/UI) pursuing a career in the tech industry as a user friendly web designer. While user interface and design are my main area of focus, I'm also working on becoming more proficient at building websites from the ground up.

An Indiana native and Fort Wayne local, I've been steeping in the artistic landscape of this city for several years now. My work history lies mostly in customer service, which extends into my UX/UI designs to help people efficiently navigate any project I have a hand in. I've been cultivating my eye for design since my teens when I was first able to get my hands on a DSLR, and more often than not can be found with one of my cameras in tow whenever I leave home.
